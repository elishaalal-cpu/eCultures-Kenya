Frequently Asked Questions

eCultures is an online platform dedicated to promoting and preserving Kenyan cultural heritage through stories, images, research, and digital exhibitions.

Kenya is home to more than 40 ethnic communities, each with unique traditions, languages, dances, and cuisines. eCultures ensures that these treasures are celebrated and shared globally.
