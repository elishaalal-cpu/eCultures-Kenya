1. Introduction

eCultures Kenya is a digital heritage platform dedicated to celebrating, preserving, and promoting the diverse cultural identities of Kenya’s forty-two ethnic communities. We connect tradition with technology by documenting stories, languages, attire, beliefs, and arts that shape who we are as Kenyans. Our platform acts as a virtual museum and learning hub that bridges the past and present for future generations.
