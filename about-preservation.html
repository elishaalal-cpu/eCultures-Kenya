How We Preserve Culture

eCultures Kenya uses digital tools such as archiving, photography, virtual storytelling, and multimedia exhibitions to safeguard Kenya’s traditions. We partner with local communities and schools to gather authentic stories, songs, and art pieces that are then digitized and made available to a global audience.

Our approach blends technology with respect — ensuring every digital record honors the people behind the story. Through workshops, documentation projects, and online learning, we empower communities to preserve their culture sustainably.
