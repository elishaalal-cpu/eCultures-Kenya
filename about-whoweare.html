Who We Are

eCultures Kenya is a digital cultural preservation initiative founded by a team of passionate storytellers, educators, and technologists who believe that culture is the foundation of identity. Our platform was built to bridge the gap between tradition and technology, ensuring that Kenya’s diverse heritage is protected and shared with the world.

We work collaboratively with cultural experts, local communities, and historians to create authentic, research-backed content that celebrates Kenya’s people and their way of life. Our mission is to make heritage accessible, engaging, and educational for all audiences.
